Our Heritage

Jubilee Christian Church Eastlands Kayole is a branch/arm of outreach of Jubilee Christian Church Eastlands which is under the leadership of Pastor Daniel Murage and Pastor Sharlet Murage. Jubilee Christian Church Eastlands is under Jubilee Christian Church which is under the leadership of our Bishop Allan Kiuna and Reverend Kathy Kiuna. Our Bishop is honorably submitted to Evangelist Teresia Wairimu of the Faith Evangelistic Ministries headquartered in Karen Nairobi Kenya. Our Senior Mother Evangelist Teresia Wairimu is /was submitted to the late Evangelist Reinhard Bonkke

We are blessed to have a rich heritage and lineage and most importantly to be entrusted by our father/Prophet/Evangelist/Missioner and Pastor Daniel Murage and Pastor Sharlet Murage to extend Gods work in Kayole and beyond as God entrusts us.

Pastor Charles and Pastor Mary are the Resident Pastors of Jubilee Christian Church Eastlands Kayole.

Mandate

Our mission in Kayole is :

  • Soul winning
  • Discipleship
  • Leadership

To fullfill the above As entrusted missioners in Kayole there are specific instructions and promises we have been entrusted with by God to fulfil the overall mission of JCC Eastlands.

  • Raising an army from bones- Like Ezekiel was asked if the bones would live, we are entrusted to speak and prophesy to people in Kayole affecting their spiritual, mental, physical lives and raise an army of soul winners, disciple makers and leaders in different fields of life. We are entrusted with the mandate of investing in people that they may entrust their lives to God through serving Him and winning souls. We invest in people for them to become soul winners. People cannot serve God well when dead, sick, burdened, oppressed, yoked, depressed or invaded by demons.
  • Gathering people to God and to Zion. We are called to bring people to God through soul winning and to Zion-Gods House through prayer, fasting, love and care, invitations, outreaches, advertisement opportunities available, calling them physically and spiritually. Gods will is for people to gather in multitude for His honor and for hell to consistently loose men.
  • Maintaining a fire Generation and sustaining them for Generations to come. We have an intercessory/ watchman assignment where we create an army of intercessors that will pray and intercede 24/7/365 until Jesus comes to maintain an open heaven in Kayole and the nation of Kenya for ease of soul winning and outreach endeavors. JCCEast Kayole is a torch of prayer, soul winning, revival and manifestations of the Spirit for all nations and we intend that the torch light up beyond this generation if Jesus tarries.
  • Raising men and Women from the dust of life to financial dignity, we are mandated to empower people financially through training, business skill improvement, getting jobs for believers and financial support as will be necessary and possible for them to move ahead and also help others later.
  • Teaching and Training of men to take territory for Jesus like David trained men to fight to get the ark back to Israel.
  • To affect the music and arts for soul winning and territory taking. We are mandated to train, support, teach, guide, invest in and dispatch music and art practitioners for more impact for Jesus.
  • To affect the relationships between men and women from their unmarried state to the time they get it o marriage and beyond. A lot has been broken in terms of foundations when it comes to the male and female relationships and family. We are mandated to heal breaches and reinstate foundations that please God and serve the overall mission of the commission. Sin came into earth because of a couple’s communication deficiency. The walls of relationships have been broken and Gods desire to repair them through us.
  • To shine forth the light that Christ has illuminated and manifested through us to Kayole and the world from our current location and position. That is through testimonials, teaching manuals, books, videos, audios on electronic and publish media.
  • Feed my lambs, tend my sheep, feed my sheep Teach all levels of believers from new believers to disciples to shepherds and teachers with the full counsel of God, answering all life’s questions and training them up to the level of being as Christ Himself.
